# Kesko Corporation — Comparable Company Analysis (Trading Comps)

A comprehensive trading comparables model for Kesko Corporation (KESKOB.HE) benchmarked against 12 publicly traded European retail/grocery peers, built following CFI Financial Modeling Best Practices and Financial Modeling Guidelines.

### Peer Selection Rationale

**Tier 1 (Highest Comparability):** Axfood and ICA are Nordic grocery leaders in mature, concentrated markets with franchise/wholesale models similar to Kesko's K-retailer network. These are the closest structural matches.

**Tier 2 (Moderate):** Tokmanni is Finnish but discount-focused. Ahold Delhaize and Tesco are global grocery leaders with comparable margin profiles but significantly larger scale and different geographic exposure.

**Tier 3 (Supplementary):** Provides European retail context across grocery (Carrefour, Jeronimo Martins, Sainsbury, Colruyt), discount (B&M), and building/DIY (Hornbach, Kingfisher) — the latter two being relevant for Kesko's 36% Building & Technical division.

### Key Insight

At peer median multiples, Kesko appears overvalued. This is the wrong conclusion. The comps analysis reveals Kesko's premium is real and persistent because the peer set includes large-cap, lower-margin grocers (Carrefour at 5.4% EBITDA margin, Tesco at 8.1%) that structurally trade at lower multiples. When narrowed to Tier 1 peers (Axfood 12.4x, ICA 14.1x EV/EBITDA), Kesko's 10.1x actually represents a discount to Nordic grocery leaders.

## Premium / Discount Justification

| Factor | Kesko | Peer Median | Assessment |
|--------|-------|------------|------------|
| EBITDA Margin | 9.1% | ~7.1% | **PREMIUM** — Superior profitability across divisions |
| Revenue Growth (LTM) | 4.7% | 2.5% | **PREMIUM** — Above-median, M&A + organic contribution |
| Net Debt / EBITDA | 0.8x | 1.5x | **PREMIUM** — Lowest leverage in peer group |
| Dividend Yield | 4.5% | 3.5% | **PREMIUM** — Above-median, 30+ year track record |
| Market Position | #1-2 Finland | Varies | **PREMIUM** — Dominant in all Finnish segments |
| Diversification | 3 divisions | 1-2 | **PREMIUM** — Grocery + Building + Car reduces volatility |
| Geographic Concentration | 60% Finland | Multi-country | **DISCOUNT** — Single-country risk |
| B&T Cyclicality | 36% of revenue | Low/None | **DISCOUNT** — Construction downturn exposure |
| Float / Liquidity | EUR 8B | EUR 13B median | **DISCOUNT** — Below-median for global investors |

**Net Assessment: 6 premium factors vs. 3 discount factors. Kesko warrants a 10-15% premium to peer median multiples.**
